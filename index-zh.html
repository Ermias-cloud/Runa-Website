我们的贸易产品

RUNA operates across two core business lines — exporting premium Ethiopian pulses & sesame seeds, and importing industrial chemicals essential to Ethiopian manufacturing.

Pinto Beans (HPS)

HPS · NON-GMO · SELECTED
Hand-picked, sun-dried Ethiopian pinto beans meeting the highest international quality standards.

White Sesame Seeds

EXPORT GRADE · FODS · SELECTED
Premium white sesame sourced from Ethiopia's fertile growing regions. Rich in oil content — ideal for food and processing industries.

Brown Sesame Seeds

BULK AVAILABLE · SELECTED
High-quality brown sesame suited for food processing and oil extraction. Selected & polished to premium standards.

Red Kidney Beans

SOUTH TYPE · HPS · SELECTED
Vibrant, firm kidney beans meeting strict HPS grading standards for global food markets.

White Pea Beans

NAVY BEAN · PREMIUM · SELECTED
Small, dense Navy beans selected & polished — ideal for canning, stews, and baked bean production.

Red Speckled Kidney Beans

PREMIUM GRADE · SELECTED
Striking speckled kidney beans selected & polished to meet international export standards for size, purity, and moisture.

Caustic Soda

SODIUM HYDROXIDE · NaOH
99% pure sodium hydroxide for textile, soap, paper, and water treatment industries. Available in flakes and pearls.

Soda Ash

SODIUM CARBONATE · Na₂CO₃
Dense & light grades of sodium carbonate for glass, detergent manufacturing, and chemical processing.

Sodium Sulphate

ANHYDROUS · Na₂SO₄
Key input for detergent, paper, and textile dyeing industries. 99% pure, supplied in bulk 25 kg bags.

Citric Acid

FOOD & INDUSTRIAL · C₆H₈O₇
Versatile monohydrate acidulant used in food processing, beverages, cleaning agents, and pharmaceuticals.

Sodium Tripolyphosphate

SOAP NOODLE GRADE · Na₅P₃O₁₀
STPP for detergent and soap noodle manufacturing — consistent 99% purity, competitive pricing.
